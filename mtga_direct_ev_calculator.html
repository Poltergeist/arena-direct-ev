Notes & assumptions

Tournament model: race — keep playing until maxWins wins or maxLosses losses, whichever comes first. Each match independent with probability p = matchWinRate.

Closed-form distribution: for k < W, P(end at k wins) = C(k+L−1, L−1) · p^k · (1−p)^L. For k = W, P = Σ_{l=0..L−1} C(W+l−1, W−1) · p^W · (1−p)^l. Sums to 1 by construction.

Reward conversion: each tier has gems + pack count + physical-prize $ value. Gem-equivalent = gems + packs · packGems + physUSD · usdGems. Toggle either component on/off; defaults match your "gems only" preference.

Pack value of 200 gems matches the gem-store pack price. 184 gems/$1 is derived from the $50 → 9,200 gems bundle (the rate gets worse on smaller bundles, so 184 is generous to physical-prize value).

Bo3 caveat: if you only know game WR, convert with p_match = p_game² · (3 − 2·p_game) before plugging in.

Risk of ruin = fraction of paths ending below the entry cost (i.e. you can't enter another event). Stats use all simulated runs.
